About Us

At JIVA , we specialize in converting agricultural by-products like banana leaves, pineapple fibers, orange peels, and corn husks into sustainable materials. Our goal is to reduce environmental impact and provide innovative alternatives for industries like textiles, biofuel, and packaging.

Our solutions

  • Eco-Friendly Fabrics: We create textiles from banana fibers, pineapple leaves, and orange peel.
  • Organic Fertilizers: Transforming crop residues into nutrient-rich compost.
  • Biofuel Production: Converting agricultural waste into renewable energy sources.
  • Biodegradable Packaging: Utilizing plant-based materials for sustainable packaging solution.

Why Choose Us

Sustainable & Eco-Friendly: Reducing carbon footprint through innovative recycling. Waste Reduction: Preventing agricultural waste from polluting the environment. Industry Partnerships: Collaborating with fashion, energy, and farming sectors. Research & Development: Continuously improving waste-to-product conversion methods.

It's Working

  1. Collection: Sourcing waste from farms and agricultural industries.
  2. Processing: Extracting useful materials through eco-friendly techniques.
  3. Manufacturing: Creating textiles, biofuels, and compost.
  4. Distribution: Supplying sustainable products to industries and consumers.
